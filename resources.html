Crisis

If someone is in immediate danger

If someone discloses imminent self-harm risk, active violence, or immediate safety threats, treat it as urgent and involve emergency services and/or crisis professionals per your context and policy.

If you’re hosting this publicly, consider adding region-specific crisis info on your own deployment.
